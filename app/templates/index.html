Welcome to the Graduate School Application Tracker

Your one-stop solution to track and manage all your graduate school applications.

Why Use GradTracker?

GradTracker simplifies the process of tracking your applications for various graduate programs. With our easy-to-use dashboard, you can:

  • Keep track of different schools and programs you have applied to.
  • Organize application deadlines and important dates.
  • Edit and update your application details as needed.
  • Manage your applications in one centralized, accessible location.
  • Track your Letters of Recommendation from professors.
  • Organize and maintain personal statements.

New Features

Exciting new features to enhance your application tracking experience:

  • Letters of Recommendation: Keep track of your recommendation letters, their status, and manage them efficiently.
  • Personal Statements: Organize your personal statements and edit them as per your application needs.

Getting Started

Getting started with GradTracker is simple:

  1. Register: Create a free account by navigating to the Registration Page.
  2. Login: Once registered, log in to access your personalized dashboard.
  3. Add Applications: Start adding your graduate school applications with all necessary details.
  4. Track and Manage: View, edit, and manage your applications, letters of recommendation, and personal statements all in one place.
